Our Mission

Greenwood Library is committed to providing access to a diverse collection of books, digital resources, and community programs that foster literacy and lifelong learning. We aim to be a cornerstone of the community, offering a welcoming space for individuals to explore, learn, and grow.

History

Established in 1990, Greenwood Library has grown from a small community library to a comprehensive resource center. Over the years, we have expanded our collections and services to meet the evolving needs of our community. We are proud to support educational and cultural enrichment through our wide range of offerings.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of librarians and staff are passionate about helping you find the resources you need. Whether you are looking for the latest bestseller, conducting academic research, or seeking recommendations for your next read, we are here to assist you.
